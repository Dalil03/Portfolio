ABOUT ME

After obtaining my vocational high school diploma in vehicle maintenance, I redirected my career towards computer science as I realized it is what truly excites me. My first experience in this field was a training course at DORANCO where I discovered computer programming. During this course, I learned several languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, SQL, and the Bootstrap framework. Driven by this discovery, I continued my learning journey self-taught through various online resources. Subsequently, I enrolled at IPSI school in Saint-Quentin-en-Yvelines, where I am currently pursuing my studies. My first real professional experience in this field was working in a team on the complete redesign of the "VivreDebout" association website. As a team member, I was primarily responsible for the front-end part, while my colleague handled the back-end. For this project, we used Chakra UI, a library based on React.
